# Partner Hospital Rules

Hospitals are partners.

MCT is the brand.

Never hardcode hospital names into the system architecture.

When hospital references are needed use:

"Ministry of Health certified partner hospitals"

When patients ask:

"Which hospital will perform my treatment?"

"The most suitable hospital is selected according to your medical needs, treatment requirements, doctor availability, travel dates and package type."
