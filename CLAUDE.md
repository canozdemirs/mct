
## Site Scope Update: Multi-Treatment Positioning (2026)

MCT homepage is no longer hair-transplant-only. It positions as the
International Patient Center for the full range below.

### Treatment Categories (see lib/treatments.ts as single source of truth)
- Hair Transplant (FUE/DHI)
- Plastic & Aesthetic Surgery: Rhinoplasty, Breast Augmentation, Liposuction, Tummy Tuck, BBL, Facelift/Necklift
- Dental: Veneers, Implants, Hollywood Smile
- Medical Check-up: Standard / VIP / Pediatric packages
- General Medical: Bariatric surgery

### Content Architecture
- Pillar page: /treatment-in-turkey
- Comparison page: /treatment-prices-in-turkey (table format, price ranges)
- Spoke pages: /treatments/[slug] with direct-answer opening, FAQ schema, 4-tier package selector, MedicalProcedure + Offer schema
